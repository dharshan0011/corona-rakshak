They have two options:

Private Healthcare
Those opting for private hospitals can avail of the facility of doorstep collection of samples. A healthcare worker will come to your home after you send the test prescriptions given by your doctor with the diagnostic lab either by e-mail or WhatsApp. With respect to private healthcare, you will have to pay for these tests out of your own pocket. Once the samples are collected, you will have to quarantine yourself immediately as a precautionary measure and wait for the results of your tests. Quarantine facilities are available at private hospitals in several cities across the country.

Public Healthcare
You will be required to contact the local health administration and inform them of your symptoms. You will then be asked to approach the Covid-19 hospital nearest to your location while maintaining physical distance with everyone
